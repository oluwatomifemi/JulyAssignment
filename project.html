Here is a time line of Einstein's life

  • 1879: Albert Einstein is born in Ulm, Germany, the first child of Hermann and Pauline Einstein.
  • 1880: The Einstein family moves to Munich, Germany.
  • 1885: Einstein enrolls in the second grade of a Catholic elementary school called the Petersschule. He receives Jewish religious instruction at home and also begins taking violin lessons.
  • 1888: Einstein is accepted at the Luitpold Gymnasium
  • 1889: The medical student Max Talmud begins joining the Einstein family regularly for dinner and introduces Albert to scientific and philosophical writings.
  • 1894: The Einstein family moves to Milan, Italy, but Einstein remains in Munich to complete his education at the Gymnasium. Later that year, drops out of the Luitpold Gymnasium and joins his family in Milan.
  • 1895: After failing the entry exam for the Zurich Polytechnic, Einstein is accepted to the trade department of the cantonal school in Aarau, Switzerland. He lives with the family of Jost Winteler, a teacher in Aarau.
  • 1896: Einstein officially renounces his German citizenship. In october, he begins studying at the Zurich Polytechnic in a teachers' training program.
  • 1900: Einstein receives his diploma as a mathematics teacher and starts work on his doctoral thesis.
  • 1901: Einstein officially becomes a Swiss citizen. In July, he gets a job to work as a temporary teacher at the Technical College in Winterthur, Switzerland. In September, Einstein works as a teacher at a private school in Schaffhausen, Switzerland.
  • 1902: Lieserl, Einstein and Mileva Maric's illegitimate daughter, is born in Hungary. Einstein is hired as a patent officer in Bern.
  • 1903: Einstein and Mileva are married in Bern. In April, Einstein forms the Olympia Academy with his friends Maurice Solovine and Conrad Habicht.
  • 1904: Hans Albert, Einstein and Mileva's first son, is born in Bern.
  • 1905: Einstein completes his paper on quantum theory. That smae year, Einstein's paper on Brownian motion is accepted by the Annalen der Physik. Einstein's paper on the special theory of relativity is published in the Annalen der Physik.
  • 1906: Einstein receives his doctorate from the University of Zurich, and becomes a privatdozent at Bern University.
  • 1909: Einstein is appointed extraordinary professor of theoretical physics at Zurich University.
  • 1912: Einstein is appointed professor of theoretical physics at Zurich Polytechnic. This is also the year Einstein begins collaborating with Marcel Grossman about the general theory of relativity.
  • 1919: A solar eclipse has allowed scientists to observe phenomena predicted by Einstein's general relativity theory, and news of the theory's confirmation is announced at a ceremonial meeting of the Royal Society of London. Einstein becomes famous.
  • 1922: Einstein wins the Nobel Prize for his work on quantum theory.
  • 1933: The Nazis seize power in Germany. Einstein resigns from the Prussian Academy of Sciences and declares that he will not return to Germany. He moves to the United States with his wife and his secretary Helen Dukas.
  • 1940: Einstein becomes an American citizen.
  • 1955: Einstein dies in New Jersey. His body is cremated and his papers turned over to the Hebrew University in Jerusalem.
